We are delighted that you are here to learn more about our company! Green Field Laboratories Ltd is nicely positioned to take full advantage of the opportunities that the British healthcare industry offers! Since our inception in 1997, we have been constantly investing in further improving our organizational capabilities and in creating Green Field Laboratories as a leading manufacturer of quality medicines in UK

In our state-of-the-art facility we manufacture a broad range of prescription and OTC products as well as vitamins and supplements. We are actively expanding our product portfolio, complementing our in-house development with a targeted licensing-in strategy which will also take Green field Laboratories into new therapeutic areas. We currently market our products in over 80,000 retail pharmacies and in 5000 hospitals. Given the quality of our products we are now also selling Green field Laboratories Laboratories brands in different surrounding regional markets.!
